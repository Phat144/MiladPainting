Spraying Painters In Melbounre

Spray painting is a technique that is extensively used these days because they are easy, quick and can be finished before the scheduled period of time. It also reduces manual labour and relieves the painters from getting stressed! Thus water based paints are becoming popular. This is to make use of spray gun instead of brush that is used by painters to paint manually. Speed, quality of finish and flexibility are the main criteria’s you got to see when selecting a method to paint. Milad Painters Melbourne are specialized in Spray painting services and can bring amazing paint results because they are the best Melbourne painters you could ever find!



Advantages of Spray Painting:

❍ Ease to do repair work
❍ Faster process
❍ It dries very quickly!
❍ Varnishes, lacquers and water based products dry very fast and so can be recoated within hours of the first coat!
❍ You would be able to apply several coats on one single day which makes this process easy.
❍ Since it dries very fast, there is very little time for the dust particles to settle on it, which eliminates sanding process costs
❍ Techniques such as toning or shading, which cannot be applied effectively by hand can be done perfectly using Spray Painting
❍ By having control over pressure of the spray gun, the air fluid flow and fan size will allow even coats thereby eliminating thick spots and brush marks
❍ Spray painting is easy to master and gives a professional finish!
❍ Problems associated with a hand painting such as drips, sags, trapped air bubbles etc are greatly reduced
❍ Spray Painting greatly saves time
❍ Spray Painting reduces stress for painters and does not hurts their back as manual painting job does
❍ Spray Painting is a very fast process that saves days of manual labor
❍ Spray Painting is very much a DIY (Do It Yourself) process so self efforts get paid!!


Disadvantages Of Spray Painting

❍ The main disadvantages of Spray Painting are
❍ Overspray
❍ Difficulty to control
❍ Spray Painting rough surfaces is tuff

They are disadvantages of course but can be overcome with proper planning and comes along with little bit of practice! Carefully observe the limitations of Spray Painting and plan the process consequently. Planning starts with the pre-painting preparation for residential painting. All other processes that you do for manual painting, you would do for Spray Painting as well!! The difference is only when the masking phase begins!

Preparation To Be Done Before Spraying Paint!

⇨ Overspray is one big problem that you would definitely face while handling Spray Paints! To protect your surroundings like furniture, windows, doors etc that are just near the area you Spray paint; completely cover them with plastic papers and tape them.
⇨ Extensive areas can be protected with drop cloths. This helps you to avoid unnecessary over spray of paint on fence, neighbour’s properties that are close to your painting area etc…
